I am a second year Ph.D. student at the School of Engineering at Universidad de los Andes advised by Prof. Mario Linares-Vásquez. I am part of The Software Design Lab (TSDL) and I am currently a visiting PhD. Student at SEART research group under the advise of Prof. Gabriele Bavota. I work on automation of software engineering tasks, quality attributes in mobile apps and software testing.
